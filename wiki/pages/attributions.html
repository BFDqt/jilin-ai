Genomic Databases

GenBank

Maintained by the National Center for Biotechnology Information (NCBI), one of the world's largest nucleic acid sequence databases.

Visit GenBank

EMBL

Maintained by the European Bioinformatics Institute (EBI), a member of the International Nucleotide Sequence Database Collaboration (INSDC).

Visit EMBL

DDBJ

Managed by the National Institute of Genetics in Japan, serves as the Asian node of INSDC.

Visit DDBJ

Analysis Tools

UCSC Genome Browser

A renowned genome browser that provides visualization and download services for genomic data.

Visit UCSC

STRING

A database for searching known and predicted protein-protein interactions.

Visit STRING

Structural Biology

AlphaFold

Developed by DeepMind, enables high-precision prediction of protein 3D structures and stands as a revolutionary tool in structural biology.

Visit AlphaFold

PDB (Protein Data Bank)

Stores experimentally determined 3D structures of biological macromolecules such as proteins and nucleic acids.

Visit PDB
