Prof. Bo Yang

Primary PI

Introduction

Bo Yang, a professor and doctoral supervisor, is a national leading talent. He is currently the dean of the School of Computer Science and Technology, the School of Software, the School of Cyber Security, and the director of the Key Laboratory of Symbolic Computing and Knowledge Engineering of the Ministry of Education at Jilin University. He obtained his doctoral degree from the School of Computer Science at Jilin University in 2003 and has visited and exchanged at Hong Kong Baptist University, the University of Warwick in the United Kingdom, the University of Technology Sydney, and George Mason University. His main research areas are artificial intelligence and knowledge engineering. His current research directions include graph machine learning and graph mining, complex system learning, neural symbolic systems, intelligent recommender systems, graph neural networks, graph optimization, and human-machine fusion systems.
